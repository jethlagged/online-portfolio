PyCon APAC 2019

Held every year, PyCon APAC is a non-profit conference dedicated to users of Python, allowing them to explore and discuss the programming language amongst their peers. It was decided that Metro Manila, Philippines was where they were going to hold their 2019 conference. Kirigami partnered with the organization, Python PH, to help bring life and personality into that year's event.

Year

2018 – 2019

Type

Brochure website

A photo of attendees taken on Day 1 (Source: PyCon Philippines)

The challenge

Branding and graphic design isn't really my strongest suit, so this section of the project easily became my biggest concern. Despite my worries, I was still determined to do my best. After multiple trial and errors, I was able to come up with a design that I and our clients were satisfied with.

The solution

The event organizers gave us complete liberty on the direction of the visual design, but they did request that it include something that would signify that the conference was held in the Philippines.

Since the copy and details on the conference came after I had started on the design, I had to anticipate what kind of information would be useful for readers to see. Because the branding was so lively and playful, it made this project very fun to work on.

I studied digital filmmaking during my undergrad studies at De La Salle- College of Saint Benilde in Manila, Philippines, where I was introduced to the other implementations of art through my involvement with a design organization. Soon after, I realized that user experience design was something I wanted to pursue as I can not only do work that I enjoy, but also help make the world a better place.

I started my UI/UX design journey in 2017, when I started working for Kirigami on their web and mobile applications, surrounded by talented and supportive people. Currently, I am looking for more ways to challenge my capabilities (like coding this website!).

When I'm not doing design stuff, I spend my time reading graphic novels, editing photos and videos, singing pop songs in a tiny voice, and thinking about my next meal.
